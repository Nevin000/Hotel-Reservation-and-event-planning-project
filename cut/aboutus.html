ABOUT US

HOTEL | PHOENIX

Welcome to Phoenix Hotels, a collection of three beautiful getaways situated around Bali, Indonesia's enchanted paradise. Discover the appeal of Denpasar, enjoy the beach splendor of Beachfront, and immerse yourself in Negara's cultural attractions. Each of our hotels provides a distinct experience, guaranteeing that your stay with Phoenix Hotels is nothing less than amazing.
Our first hotel, located in the center of Denpasar, embodies the dynamic spirit of Bali's busy capital city. As you enter our exquisite lobby, you will be greeted by a combination of modern design and traditional Balinese characteristics. Every aspect at Denpasar Hotel is designed to offer a quiet refuge amidst the urban hum, from the exquisite service of our dedicated personnel to the precisely created rooms and suites.
Our Beachfront Hotel, nestled along Bali's pristine shores, offers a beautiful hideaway where turquoise waves meet golden dunes. Imagine waking up to the sound of breaking waves and magnificent vistas of the ocean spread out in front of you. Relax and unwind in our beautifully designed rooms and suites, each with a private balcony or patio overlooking the beach. At our seaside restaurant, delectable tastes and fresh seafood combine to offer a unique dining experience.
Our third hotel welcomes you to enjoy Bali's rich cultural heritage in the lovely village of Negara. A refuge of calm awaits you here, amidst the beautiful tropical sceneries and peaceful surroundings. Every part of our Negara Hotel exudes a combination of contemporary luxury and traditional Balinese charm. Immerse yourself in the tranquillity of our rooms and suites, each of which has been created to mirror the peacefulness of the surrounding countryside.
Each of our hotels adopts eco-friendly programs as part of our commitment to sustainable tourism, ensuring that your stay contributes to the preservation of Bali's natural beauty. Our experienced staff would be glad to assist you in creating customised tours that will allow you to discover Bali's hidden gems and create cherished experiences that will last a lifetime.
Phoenix Hotels invites you to experience the enchantment of Bali. Whether you stay downtown Denpasar, enjoy Beachfront splendor, or immerse yourself in Negara's cultural charms, our hotel collection offers a wonderful vacation that will captivate you. Allow us to be your portal to Bali's vibrant spirit, where paradise becomes a reality and cherished experiences unfold.
